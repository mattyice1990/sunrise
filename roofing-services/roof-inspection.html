What's Included in Our Roof Inspection

Our comprehensive roof inspection covers every aspect of your roofing system, identifying potential issues before they become expensive problems.

Detailed Surface Inspection

We examine all roofing materials for cracks, missing pieces, wear patterns, and UV damage specific to Arizona's climate.

Leak Detection

Thorough check for water penetration, moisture damage, and potential leak points including valleys and penetrations.

Structural Assessment

Evaluation of decking condition, support structure, and signs of sagging or structural concerns.

Ventilation Check

Assessment of attic ventilation, crucial for Tucson's extreme heat and proper roof system function.

Flashing & Seals

Inspection of all flashing around chimneys, vents, skylights, and other roof penetrations.

Drainage Evaluation

Check gutters, downspouts, and drainage systems to ensure proper water flow during monsoons.

Why Get a Free Roof Inspection

Annual Inspections Save Money

Regular roof inspections catch small problems before they become expensive emergencies. A minor repair today can prevent a costly replacement tomorrow.

Before Monsoon Season

Tucson's monsoon season brings intense storms. A pre-monsoon inspection identifies vulnerabilities that could lead to water damage during heavy rains.

After Severe Weather

High winds, hail, and intense sun can damage roofs. Post-storm inspections help you file insurance claims if needed.

Before Buying or Selling

Real estate transactions require accurate roof condition information. Our inspection provides the documentation you need.

Insurance Requirements

Many insurance policies require regular roof inspections, especially for older roofs. We provide documentation for your insurance company.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the roof inspection really free? +

Yes, absolutely. We provide comprehensive roof inspections at no cost with no obligation. Our inspectors don't work on commission, so you get honest recommendations without sales pressure.

How long does a roof inspection take? +

Most inspections take 45-90 minutes depending on your roof's size and complexity. We take the time needed to thoroughly evaluate every aspect of your roofing system.

Do I need to be home during the inspection? +

We prefer you're home so we can discuss findings immediately and answer questions. However, if necessary, we can inspect your roof's exterior and provide a detailed report afterward.

How often should I have my roof inspected? +

We recommend annual inspections for Arizona homes due to extreme weather conditions. Also inspect after severe storms, before monsoon season, and if you notice any signs of damage.

Will you provide a written report? +

Yes. We provide a detailed written report with photos documenting our findings. If repairs or replacement are recommended, we include a transparent itemized estimate.
