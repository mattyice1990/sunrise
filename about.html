Our Story

Founded in 2004, Sunrise Roofers LLC has been serving the Tucson and Phoenix communities for over 20 years. We started as a small family business with a commitment to quality and customer satisfaction. Today, we're proud to be one of Arizona's most trusted roofing contractors, with hundreds of successful projects and satisfied customers.

Our team of licensed professionals brings decades of combined experience to every project, ensuring your roof is built to withstand Arizona's harsh desert climate. We take pride in our work and are committed to providing honest, reliable service to every customer.
